CHECK OUT THE TRAILERS

Midsommar

A group of American friends travels to Sweden to attend a midsummer festival that only occurs once every 90 years. As the celebrations escalate, they find themselves trapped in a sinister and increasingly surreal nightmare.

The Babadook

A single mother and her son are terrorized by a mysterious creature called the Babadook, which manifests itself through a creepy children's book.

The Witch

A family in 1630s New England is torn apart by supernatural forces, including witchcraft, possession, and paranoia.

Hereditary

After the matriarch of the Graham family passes away, her daughter's family begins to unravel cryptic and increasingly terrifying secrets about their ancestry.

Get Out

A young African American man visits his white girlfriend's family estate and becomes ensnared in a more sinister real reason for the invitation.

The Conjuring

Paranormal investigators Ed and Lorraine Warren work to help a family terrorized by a dark presence in their farmhouse.

The Exorcist

When a teenage girl is possessed by a mysterious entity, her mother seeks the help of two priests to save her daughter.

Oculus

A woman tries to exonerate her brother, who was convicted of murder, by proving that the crime was committed by a supernatural phenomenon.

Nine Dead

Nine strangers are abducted and held hostage in a room with no way out. A mysterious voice informs them that they must figure out why they were brought together and killed one by one.

Creep

A videographer answers an ad for a one-day job in a remote mountain town, but soon finds out that his client has some unsettling plans for him.

A Beautiful Mind

A brilliant mathematician struggles with schizophrenia and his wife tries to help him as they navigate his illness and the challenges of academic life.

V/H/S

A group of criminals are hired to retrieve a VHS tape from a remote house, but when they arrive, they discover a collection of disturbing and horrific videos.

It Follows

A teenage girl is pursued by a supernatural entity after a sexual encounter with her boyfriend.

Sinister

A true-crime writer moves his family into a house where a family was murdered, and discovers a box of home movies that reveal supernatural forces at work.

Halloween

A masked killer stalks and murders teenagers on Halloween night in a small town.

The Blair Witch Project

A group of documentary filmmakers disappear while investigating a local legend about a witch in the woods.

Insidious

A family is haunted by malevolent spirits after their son falls into a coma and becomes a vessel for supernatural entities.

The Ring

A journalist investigates a cursed videotape that causes anyone who watches it to die seven days later.

Psycho

A secretary on the run hides out at a motel run by a strange young man with an even stranger mother.

Ju-On: The Grudge

A curse placed on a house in Japan causes anyone who enters it to become possessed by vengeful spirits.

Knock Knock

A married man's infidelity is punished when two young women show up at his door and subject him to a night of psychological torture.
